## 1. Conceptual Overview

The **Mirror Function** treats AI hallucinations as **epistemic signals**, revealing distortions in human cognition, culture, institutions, and geopolitical assumptions.  

**Objectives:**
- Extract actionable insights from hallucination events
- Update protocol rules and taxonomy
- Enable distributed, versioned science execution
